Repost Assistant Extension


How to use

First, configure and save the crawler and target rules in options page Second, open the website that involved in crawler rules Third, right click to open menu, move to 'Assistant Of Repost', click 'Repost' menu item at second level, and chrome will open the new target tab. Text content will inject into the new target tab, and if there are images in crawler page, a dialog of images-upload will open, you can select some or all images to upload and save them into target site. Therefore, you finally click the 'replace' button to replace the images' urls to local urls.
